Go to analyze page

How to use:

  1. Prepare a dataset(.csv) for which you want to examine co-occurrence.
    *In below, we are offering sample data of Titanic surviver!
  2. Press the button above and go to analyze page.
  3. Press the "File select" button. and...
  4. Open the csv file prepared in step 1.
  5. Select at least two columns for which you want to examine co-occurrence.
  6. Press the “Plot graph!" button.
  7. Your 3D co-occurrence network will be shown and ready to analyze.
In 3D figure out put, the node and edge color depth express how the importance of the results. And if you touch the items, you can check the detail inside.
*Attention to smartphone user: For keep camera perspective, tap again inside of the plot area after you controled. (Now we are fixing this problem...)

Technical:

This network is constructed on Simpson coefficient.
The values brought from the equation below.(A and B are specific elements group)
Simpson = A ∩ B / min(A, B)
In this network, higher simpson values expressed as deeper blue colors.
